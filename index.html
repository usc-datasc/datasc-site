DataSC

A club aimed at creating an approachable and diverse environment catered towards the exploration and practical applications of Data Science here at USC!

About Us

Our Mission: To foster an environment in which individuals across subjects and experience levels are able to learn Data Science and collaboratively engage in exploring cross-disciplinary topics through its practical applications.

Learn

Regardless of your experience with Data Science, there is a place for you at DataSC! We have a ground up curriculum that will walk you through setup, theory, and application.

Moreover, while the framework for a complete beginner exists, members aren't restricted by it and our curriculum has taken into account the different levels of experience members will have when joining.

Compete

If you're feeling confident in your abilities then competitions and projects are avenues in which you can try your hand at applying your knowledge!

Members can form teams to pursue projects in fields of their interest and/or take part in team based competitions, such as those on Kaggle or DataCamp. There are no hard and fast restrictions and members have the freedom to decide on what and how things can be approached!

Events and Workshops

We aim to host speaker sessions and panel events with industry leaders like NVIDIA, Microsoft, Google, and Meta; and will provide career support through things such as interview workshops, headshot taking, and networking formation and support.

Socials

Despite CS and Math major stereotypes, we plan to host actually fun social events that don't feel like management has asked us to plan a team bonding exercise
