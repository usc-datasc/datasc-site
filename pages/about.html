Our Mission: To foster an environment in which individuals across subjects and experience levels are able to learn Data Science and collaboratively engage in exploring cross-disciplinary topics thorugh its practical applications.

Compete

If you're feeling confident in your abilities then competitions and projects are avenues in which you can try your hand at applying your knowledge!

Members can form teams to pursue projects in fields and subjects of interest and/or take part in competitions such as those on Kaggle.com -- there are no hard and fast restrictions and members have the freedom to decide on what and how things can be approached!

Events and Workshops

We aim to host speaker sessions and panel events with industry leaders like NVIDIA, Microsoft, Google, and Meta; and will provide career support through things such as interview workshops, headshot taking, and networking formation and support.

Socials

Despite CS and Math major stereotypes, we will host actually fun social events on a regular but not-overly-frequent basis
